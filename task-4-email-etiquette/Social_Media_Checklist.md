## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

### ✅ Do’s (Responsible Practices)

- We should maintain privacy settings on our accounts to control who can see our posts and personal information.  
- We should do networking politely by sending personalized connection requests.  
- We should give proper credit when sharing others’ content.  
- We should Use clear and respectful language in all digital interactions.  
- We must keep our profile professional, especially on platforms used for academics or career building. We must build a         positive digital footprint.  
- We must verify information before sharing and must avoid fake news.  
- We must respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.  

---

### ❌ Don’ts (Things to Avoid)

- We must not share passwords or sensitive information with anyone, even if they are close to us.  
- We should not use social media excessively during study time.  
- We don;t have to engage with trolls or participate in cyberbullying.  
- We should never spread unverified rumours or clickbait misinformation.  
- We must avoid using weak passwords or the same password across multiple platforms.  
- We should avoid using informal or unprofessional language in public posts.  
- We must not overshare personal life details.  
- vWe have to avoid clicking on suspicious links or ads.  

---

## Key Reminder

Responsible social media use helps build a positive online identity and ensures digital safety.
