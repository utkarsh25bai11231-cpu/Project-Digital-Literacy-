Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist      

✅ Do’s (Responsible Practices)
•	Maintain privacy settings on your accounts to control who can see your posts and personal information.
•	Do networking politely by sending personalized connection requests.
•	Give proper credit when sharing others’ content.
•	Do use clear, respectful language in all digital interactions.
•	Keep your profile professional, especially on platforms used for academics or career building. Try to build positive digital footprint.
•	Verify information before sharing and avoid fake news.
•	Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.
                                                       



❌ Don’ts (Things to Avoid)
•	Do not share passwords or sensitive information to anyone, even though they may be very close to you.
•	Do not use social media excessively during study time.
•	Don't engage with trolls or participate in cyberbullying.
•	Don't spread unverified rumours or "clickbait" misinformation.
•	Avoid using weak passwords or the same password at multiple digital platforms.
•	Avoid using informal or unprofessional language in public posts.
•	Don’t overshare personal life details.
•	Avoid clicking on suspicious links or ads.

We must remember:
             Responsible social media use helps us to build a 
             positive online identity. Also, it ensures digital        
             safety.
